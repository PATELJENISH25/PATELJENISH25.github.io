Spicy Chicken Curry

This delicious spicy chicken curry is made with a blend of aromatic spices, including cumin, coriander, and turmeric. Serve it with rice or naan for a satisfying meal.

Ingredients: Chicken, onions, tomatoes, garlic, ginger, cumin, coriander, turmeric, chili powder, salt, oil

Instructions:start by heating oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Add chopped onions and cook until translucent, then stir in minced garlic and ginger, cooking for another minute. Next, add diced tomatoes and cook until softened. Stir in ground cumin, coriander, turmeric, and chili powder, cooking for an additional 2 minutes. Add chicken pieces to the skillet and brown on all sides. Pour in water or chicken broth to cover the chicken, bring to a boil, then reduce heat and simmer for 20-25 minutes until the chicken is fully cooked. Season with salt to taste and serve hot with rice or naan bread for a delicious and flavorful meal.

Vegetable Biryani

This aromatic vegetable biryani is a classic Indian dish made with basmati rice, mixed vegetables, and a blend of spices. It's perfect for a hearty vegetarian meal.

Ingredients: Basmati rice, mixed vegetables (carrots, peas, potatoes), onions, tomatoes, ginger, garlic, garam masala, saffron, nuts, raisins, oil

Instructions: start by rinsing the rice under cold water until it runs clear, then soak it in water for 30 minutes. In a large pot over medium heat, heat ghee and sauté whole spices until fragrant. Add sliced onions and cook until golden brown, then add ginger-garlic paste and sauté until the raw smell disappears. Next, add chopped vegetables and cook until they begin to soften. Drain the soaked rice and add it to the pot, gently stirring to combine with the vegetables. Pour in water or vegetable broth, season with salt to taste, and bring to a boil. Once boiling, reduce the heat to low, cover the pot, and let simmer until the rice is cooked and the liquid is absorbed. Garnish with chopped cilantro and fried onions, then serve hot with raita or yogurt on the side. Enjoy your flavorful Vegetable Biryani!
